AYF'17 CULTURAL EVENTS





APHESIA - BATTLE OF BANDS





“For us there’s U2 music and then there’s everything else’- Adam Clayton.

APHESIA, a battle ground of soaring music bands strings together a rhapsody of ecstatic euphony. We welcome you to brighten the fest with your talent.

Registration Fees: INR 200.00 per head (including techriders and managers).

Registration fees are non-refundable.

Dates of Event: 16th, 17th, 18th February 2017

Rewards: INR 12,000 for the winners and INR 8,000 for the runners up.

 

Eligibility:

1. All participants should be from a certified institute and must carry their institutional ID cards on the day of event for registration.

  1. The competition is for current students only; professional artists are not allowed. Any involvement of such will lead to disqualification.

  2. All participants are required to report at the registration desk, an hour prior to the scheduled time.

  3. Any misconduct by contestants will lead to on spot disqualification.

  4. Multiple entries from every college are allowed.

  5. Each group is required to have a group name.

 

Round 1 (16th, 17th February)

  • Registered Teams will be escorted to H block courtyard, the venue for the event prior to the start of event.

  • Teams will be allotted 10 minutes for their performance including sound check. Exceeding the time limit would lead to negative marking.

  • Teams are to perform cover songs and atleast one OC (original composition).

  • Judgment will be made on basis of originality/creativity, musical clarity, stage performance, crowd interaction and time management.

  • Results will be announced after the performances of all registered teams. Of all the teams performing, only five teams will be shortlisted for the final round.

  • Care should be taken not to violate any form of copyrights.

  • Drum kit will be provided, which would include one crash and ride cymbal each. Any other instruments required should be carried by the teams. Teams are suggested to carry their own cables for processors.

  • Failure to show up on venue at the allotted time will result in disqualification.

  • Decision of the judges will be final and irrevocable.

  • Any distasteful behavior, and or damage to AUUP will result in an automatic disqualification at the hands of management and judges.

 

Round 2 (18th February, 2017)

  • The shortlisted five teams will be performing on the main stage, (The venue for the final round).

  • 10 (Ten minutes) will be allotted to each team as performance time.

  • All rules and regulations as of last round stand the same for this round.

  • Judgments will be made on the same grounds.

  • Original Compositions will be given higher preference than cover songs.

  • Infringement of copyright laws will lead to immediate disqualification.

  • Decision of the judges will be final and irrevocable.

  • Any distasteful behavior, and or damage to AUUP will result in an automatic disqualification at the hands of management and judges.

 

Judging Scale and Criteria:

  1. Music (This criteria is based on clarity, lyrical content and overall musical performance) 1-10

  2. Stage Presence and Crowd Interaction (This Criteria is based on how well the band "rocks out" and how well the band connects with the crowd) 1-5

    3) Appearance and Personality (This criterion is based on the overall look and personality. If you want to be a rock star, you need to look the part!)1-5

    4) Crowd Applause (The key ingredient to rock stardom: THE FANS!)1-20

“MAY THE BEST TEAM WIN THE WAR AND THE CROWD!”
