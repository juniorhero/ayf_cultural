AYF'17 CULTURAL EVENTS





OSCILLATIONS





“Dance, when you are broken open. Dance, if you’ve torn the bandage off. Dance in the middle of the fighting. Dance when you are perfectly free” –Rumi.

OSCILLATIONS is a dias bringing to you an euphoric combat of western group dancing. Come, fire the stage with your modern grooves.

 

Eligibility

  • All participants should be from a certified institute and must carry their institutional ID cards on the day of event for registration.

  • The competition is for current students only; professional artists are not allowed. Any involvement of such will lead to disqualification.

  • Teams can perform any type of Western Dances.

  • Team size: minimum 6 members to a maximum of 22 members.

  • All participants are required to report at the registration desk, an hour prior to the scheduled time.

  • Any misconduct by contestants will lead to on spot disqualification.

  • Multiple entries from every college are allowed.

  • Each group is required to have a group name.

Team Registration

  • The teams should register and complete the registration forms available at amity.edu/ayf/cultural, before the given last date.

  • You will be provided with a team ID which is to be used for further communication with any member of Amity’s organizing committee.

  • You will be notified regarding the event timings and schedules through the given web-address and email.

  • Registration fee: Rs. 200/- per team member.

  • It is mandatory for choreographer and tech. person to be registered (max. 2 people are allowed).

  • Carry a pen-drive and CD containing your piece of music that is to be submitted to the organizing team an hour earlier.

  • Judge’s decision is final in case of any disputes, complaints or controversies arising in context of AYF.

Awards & Prizes

All participants will be provided with a certificate of participation

 

Competition Process

 

  • First Round

  • All the registered teams should reach Amity University, Noida (U.P.) gate no. 4 on 16th February 2017, by 10am.

  • Each team will be provided only 10 minutes for their performance; the 10 minutes include 8 minutes of performance and 2 minutes of set up time.

 

  • Final Round

  • All the finalist will be performing on the main stage on 17th February 2017 at Amity University, Noida (U.P.), reporting timing will be 10am.

  • They have to report backstage an hour prior to the performance.

  • Winner and runners up will be rewarded on venue by judges/chief guest.

 

NOTE

Not abiding by the rules will be subject to immediate disqualification.

 

Songs: Songs can be in of any language; song selection should be appropriate i.e. avoid any use of abusive language.

 

Props/Costumes: Teams will have to bring their own props; any assistance to set up propos will be provided (you will not be provided with any props). Appropriate costumes reflecting the dance form must be worn; changing rooms will be provided.

 

Timings:

  • Each team will be provided only 10 minutes for their performance; the 10 minutes include 8 minutes of performance and 2 minutes of set up time.

  • Time is of importance and exceeding the time limit will cost the team points; for every minute exceeding will result in negative marking.

  • Teams are expected to report backstage, an hour prior to the event.

  • Failure to report at the proper time will lead to forfeiture you team’s chance to perform.

Evaluation and Scoring

  • A panel of professional judges from outside Amity will be evaluating performances based on the following criteria:
    • Originality/ Innovation

    • Performance/Expression

    • Coordination/ Synchronization

    • Costume/ Props

    • Song Selection

    • Choreography

    • Overall impact/ Connection with audience

  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
